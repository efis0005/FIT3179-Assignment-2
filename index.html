AUSTRALIAN EDUCATION SNAPSHOT 2021

FIT3179 | EZEKIEL FISCHER | DOMAIN: EDUCATION

This visualisation aims to elucidate the trends and notable facts of Australias educational outcomes. Through an analysis of school location and performance,private and public schooling styles and school-finishing rates based on local government area (LGA) this visualisation hopes to provide a broad view of the current state of the system.
